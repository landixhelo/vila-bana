About Vila Bana

Located within 600 metres of Borsh Beach and 700 metres of Qeparo Beach, Vila Bana provides rooms with air conditioning and a private bathroom in Himare. This beachfront property offers access to a balcony, free private parking and free WiFi. The apartment features family rooms.

Overlooking the sea, units in this apartment are accessible via private entrance and fitted with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower and a hair dryer. A fridge and kettle are also provided. At the apartment complex, all units are fitted with bed linen and towels.
